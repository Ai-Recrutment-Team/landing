How It Works
From Posting to Hiring in 4 Steps
A streamlined workflow powered by AI at every stage of the recruitment pipeline.
1

Create & Publish Job Postings

Recruiters create detailed job listings with requirements, salary ranges, and custom application questions. AI assistant Milo can help draft postings with optimal language.

2

Applicants Discover & Apply

Candidates browse, search, and filter job postings. They apply directly with resumes and answer custom questions — all tracked in real-time.

3

AI Analyzes & Ranks Candidates

Milo AI instantly analyzes CVs, extracts structured data, scores candidates 0-100 against job requirements, and enables bulk comparison across all applicants.

4

Schedule, Interview & Hire

Schedule interviews with calendar integration (Google, Outlook), manage the pipeline through stages, and track every metric from application to hire.
